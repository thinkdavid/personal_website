Medellin

Getting Around:

  • Taxis here use meters and are everywhere, just ask to use them if they don’t turn them on
  • Download Didi from the app store. It’s like uber for Colombia. Cheaper than taxis, but taxis just know the city so well its fun in a way to tell them your complex and they just know how to get there no problems no questions asked.
  • Download Rappi from the app store. It’s like uber eats for Colombia. But better. You can even buy beer/liquor from here.

Neighborhoods

  • El Poblado - Where all the expats live. Super nice area though. A lot of nice bars here, walking around is super cool because of all the nature around (big trees/rivers through the city) that made it almost seem like you were in a jungle

Things to Do:

I unfortunately couldn’t do too much in Medellin because it was shut down due to COVID. So these will be things I was suggested to do by my Paisa friends.

  • Guatapé - I actually went here. Rent a car (or take a bus) and its cool to see a more quaint village in Antioquia. Beautiful views from around there. A good hostel to stay in would be Casa Encuentro - check this
  • Parque Arvi
  • Comune 13 (trece) - Graffiti tour. Go during the day, it’s a bad neighborhood.
  • Haciendo Napoles - former home of Pablo Escobar. Now it is a zoo. Interesting if you care about Narcos.

Good for Food and Drink

  • Carmen: Top tier food. Expensive for Medellin but VERY good. From the staff, to the presentation, to the ambience, definitely worth going to if you want a fancy dinner you can’t afford in the US.

Good for Food

  • El Taxista: Hole in the wall restaurant only open in the morning and afternoon. Super cheap. Super good. One of the places that has a fixed menu and just gives you a tray of food.
  • Cafetera Restaurante la Casa de Beto - Similar to taxista.

Good for Drink

  • BBC - Pub Medellin - Good for beer, casual hangout
  • Envy Rooftop - Beautiful views. Upscale.
  • OCI.mde - Nice vibes. Good food.
  • Click Clack Hotel - If you can get on the roof its got a great vibe
