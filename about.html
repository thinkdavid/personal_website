About Me:

Hi, I’m David.

Musician, Photographer, Traveler, Software Engineer
From Virginia (757), but currently in Los Angeles, CA

Read about some of my travels here! I tend to travel alone since I’ve realized that’s the best way to get out of your comfort zone and really get to know a new place and culture. I also enjoy playing piano and writing music. I’ve gotten part time gigs playing at hospitals in various cities in which I’ve lived. I also like photography and have gotten somewhat proficient at software such as Photoshop to realize some creative visions that I have. In any freetime I have left, I volunteer for the IRC as a tutor and mentor helping refugees acclimate and achieve their full potential in the US.

I work as a Software Engineer at Microsoft, primarily in Golang and F# but have experience in Swift, C#, and Python and with Docker, Kubernetes, xCode/iOS, and Azure.
